My Poem

Baking

In the kitchen, a dance of delight, Where ingredients blend, embracing the light. Flour and sugar, a sweet symphony, Creating magic, for all to see.

Mixing bowls spin, like partners in grace, As memories form, in this sacred space. Butter and eggs, harmoniously entwined, Whispering secrets, recipes defined.

Heat embraces, as ovens hum, Transforming raw batter into something yum. The scent of vanilla fills the air, Promising joy, beyond compare.

From cookies to cakes, and pies so divine, Baking brings comfort, in every line. A taste of love, in every bite, Baking captures moments, day or night.
